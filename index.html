How To Play

  1. You and the Dealer start with $1000 dollars.
  2. Bet in increments of $25, $100, $500 per hand or go all in.
  3. The last player with money left wins!
  4. Game is played with 8 decks.
  5. Dealer must hit until reaching 17, then must stand.
  6. Double down after split is allowed.
  7. You may split an unlimited number of times.
  8. For full blackjack rules, see the Wizard of Odds.
